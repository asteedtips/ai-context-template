Step 2 — Match Invoice in Zoho Books

The function uses the extracted invoice number to search Zoho Books via GET /invoices?invoice_number={extracted}. It validates: invoice exists, status is Open or Overdue, and payment amount matches the balance due.

VALIDATION PIPELINE
1. Invoice Exists? arrow_forward 2. Status = Open/Overdue? arrow_forward 3. Amount = Balance Due? arrow_forward Record Payment
If any step fails, the email is flagged in the activity log with the failure reason. No partial payments — amount must equal balance due exactly.
